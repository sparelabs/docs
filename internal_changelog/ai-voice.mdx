### üìÖ Improved Date Recognition for AI Voice

**What's New**  
AI Voice is now better at understanding relative and ambiguous dates spoken by riders. By providing the AI with two weeks of calendar context during a call, it can more accurately process phrases like "yesterday" or "next Tuesday."

**Pre-requisites**
- Requires `LaunchAIVoice` feature flag

<Accordion title="See More">

#### Where You'll See It
- This is a backend improvement to the AI Voice system. You'll notice its effects in the accuracy of trip bookings made via AI Voice, especially when riders use relative dates.

#### What You Can Do
- When a rider calls on a Wednesday and asks to book a trip for "yesterday at 2 PM," the system will now correctly recognize this as Tuesday at 2 PM.
- Previously, ambiguous date formats (e.g., a rider saying "three two" for a date) could be misinterpreted (e.g., as March 2nd instead of February 3rd). The new context helps the AI correctly identify the intended date.

#### Why This Matters
This change reduces booking errors caused by misinterpreted dates, leading to a more reliable and accurate experience for riders using AI Voice. It ensures that the date a rider speaks is the date that gets booked.
</Accordion>
