In this article, you will find answers to the following questions:

- How do I view existing fleet settings?
- How do I view which services are linked to the fleet?
- How do I re-assign a driver to a different fleet?

## View Existing Fleets Detailed Information

Go to the **Service Planning** tab and select **Fleets**. Click on the **Fleet record** to view its information.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388032/e01ac04962a57c2eb5b11dcc/MgLpU8Pg5oWSvTcsLoLypg7c0dHN7IgjYw.png?expires=1753820100&signature=b2ce9cd01329e68d2827d2a47cb0610d520d6b11bd8b709063b6dd79f5f99275&req=fSYgFcF2nYJdFb4f3HP0gO4bfaHYBDQ0qIqvb8eog2wBQn7lMWB1lOFuZqF%2B%0AK0w%3D%0A)

You will then see the fleet details, including its status, Driver Wait Time settings, and Break Policy (if applicable).

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388042/287c3f865c6953ea9200646e/gaQxj7_-2ojQgFwZRFZBSJGMDHMxt6CLXA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=9ec16592b8c816e8267f918fd1c7c6247bfbf81b7ef88a98e5ce3d5b6e3b0592&req=fSYgFcF2nYVdFb4f3HP0gBfEHYXWXzdFFlgX%2BWzill%2BPXeZOD0J20huBMfo4%0Aqoc%3D%0A)

## View Linked Services Information

From the Fleet Information Page, scroll down to see the Services Linked to This Fleet.

Click on the service line to see its details.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388051/3cf3e2e63c3d9441eb36bc0e/hRnDA28OVDtiHiM6EMhtko3Hm1g_Q5fGnA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=0321b6bb189642bdc9066e5ff42a0dd96d62efaf9ef153cdceca69b9480dec5c&req=fSYgFcF2nYReFb4f3HP0gKybCUN%2BxE33lAtM3FueO7irUeYBWy44xCkSH8pR%0AHGM%3D%0A)

You will then be redirected to the **Service Information page**.

Explore the service settings to learn about their settings, such as boarding times, rider types, fare rules, flexibilities, and other information.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388057/ee36125400320a4204a7f18c/NXT-w-G2JdOWrOeBeihZDJ0QqiB7TDW5Jg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=c249f8a38a1870132722f5f2262dbdf10add4cf1df6e5782d79e8ff2b09402d8&req=fSYgFcF2nYRYFb4f3HP0gIA%2BNjkUHvJAblZKg4CYhWsjQkbVFLhE0%2FJkoKHI%0AsSc%3D%0A)

## Re-assign Drivers

To assign a driver to another fleet, go to the **Drivers** tab (**Users** > **Drivers**) and click on the **driver record**. You will then be redirected to the **Driver Information page**.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388071/2743b941b9cad326348aa670/lUSu0mIxwi4MlRGXOx9BRVgaIfGPect2GA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=14061946a875ba7ea5020f7b987718ccd312e3b6ce04abe74fb1b449dc9d3795&req=fSYgFcF2nYZeFb4f3HP0gOvtbLYYo50%2BIb85VfV0epcHcvSK%2BAT5j7KmOOfo%0AHHw%3D%0A)

From there, click **Edit Driver** to go into the Edit Driver Form.

Start **typing a new fleet name** and select the fleet from the drop-down menu.

Click **Save** to accept the changes.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/967388079/fa9422025924215df15486fe/LFUrn_QQt1GWTNBBZ1teJ0UxZ-iqj9d25Q.png?expires=1753820100&signature=fbca4647b68cd8bf079ea46a1f81aa0bb0ef605a302f19051ffd273447b71e2b&req=fSYgFcF2nYZWFb4f3HP0gC5J1tgwifFDyWETvAgAcW0TYQndWd8fsLetc%2BuX%0AF8U%3D%0A)
