## What are Vehicle Inspections?

Many countries including the US and Canada have regulations that require public transit agencies to regularly inspect their vehicles to ensure they're fit for service. Spare's Vehicle Inspections feature allows agencies to digitize their current pre and post duty vehicle inspection processes with an inspection list that's presented to operators in Spare's Driver App before their duty starts and after it's complete.

## Completing Inspections in Spare Driver

When a driver logs into the driver app they will be prompted to start their inspection.

Drivers cannot start their duty until they have completed their inspection. 

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1074846285/c898f3f5058ce77762e2f7d7/Screenshot+2024-06-07+at+3_24_45%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1753820100&signature=42e680d35f7a9caf77ec596fde954016d83323550d289e85bf2fd344af98ef9a&req=dSAgEsF6m4NXXPMW1HO4zQyX7S0JegZOjBAh6ws2W5XLl3BTKPx2WkG6YeHV%0A7l6I%0A)

Upon pressing "Start Inspection" the drivers will be shown the Pre-Duty inspection checklist associated with the Vehicle Type of the Vehicle they will be driving. 

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1074848121/35a71d49b655bab98028047f/image.png?expires=1753820100&signature=c9f310e6983d7319985a721fc0df3a603831439291c208a70231fcd19c622c85&req=dSAgEsF6lYBdWPMW1HO4zW44WeQ%2B3AhfY8E4yXK7%2FWGIUSpNFIv4oDcXZjc8%0AFbFe%0A)

After the inspection is complete, they are given the option to "Start Driving". This begins their duty. 

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1074850567/a79718fa5e8590edc6cccabd/image.png?expires=1753820100&signature=9ba78d9772a2d0607c8d0430ff2156031070154844fc17bcdbcb57e57ca70c45&req=dSAgEsF7nYRZXvMW1HO4zaKQnBJj2KJT2rsZo%2BOTegv4UpeBPEo%2F4rxmvq%2Fm%0AYRI3%0A)

At the end of the duty, the driver will be navigated to the duty end location. When they are within 50m of the duty's end location, they will see the option to **Stop Driving**. Clicking this button will open the post-duty inspection report. 

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1074852678/6e643717f7210ba615d86b0b/image.png?expires=1753820100&signature=188d04aed72de375c123726c03b9e1dbd187af9ae92141618d7da44cdc4a5395&req=dSAgEsF7n4dYUfMW1HO4zYV1mQAaxFtRh%2BhozzLxddYmLbncNrav0hjvkSwq%0A8%2F4i%0A)
