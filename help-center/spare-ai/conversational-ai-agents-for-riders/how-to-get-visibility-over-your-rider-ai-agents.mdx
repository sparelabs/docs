## How to track AI Voice & Chat KPIs

![Spare Analytics dashboard showing AI Voice KPIs and metrics](/images/spare-ai/conversational-ai-agents/spare-ai-analytics-dashboard.png)

<Info>
KPIs relating to AI Voice can be tracked on Spare Analytics (shown above). This dashboard provides comprehensive insights into your AI agent performance.
</Info>

### Available KPIs

The following key performance indicators are available for monitoring your AI agents:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Calls" icon="phone">
    Total number of calls made to AI Voice
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Duration" icon="clock">
    Total duration of calls with IVR actions (excluding time spent connected to a customer service representative)
  </Card>
  <Card title="CSR Forwarded Calls" icon="user-headset">
    Total number of calls where a user requested to be forwarded to a customer service representative (CSR)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Booking Count" icon="calendar-plus">
    Total number of times the booking flow was initiated
  </Card>
  <Card title="ETA Count" icon="map-pin">
    Total number of times the ETA flow was initiated
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cancellation Count" icon="calendar-minus">
    Total number of times the cancellation flow was initiated
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Unique Users" icon="users">
    Total number of unique users that have used AI Voice
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to review AI Voice calls

![Interface showing how to review AI Voice calls on rider profiles](/images/spare-ai/conversational-ai-agents/spare-ai-call-review-interface.png)

<Note>
All AI Voice calls are recorded and can be reviewed on a rider's profile for quality assurance and troubleshooting purposes.
</Note>

### Steps to review AI Voice calls

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Rider Profile">
    Navigate to and select the specific rider's profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate IVR Call Metrics">
    Scroll down to **IVR Call Metrics** under the **Overview** tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Call Recording">
    Select one of the calls from the list. Calls labeled `AI Voice` are handled by the AI Voice agent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Call Details">
    Listen to the recording and review call metrics and outcomes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use call reviews to identify common issues, improve FAQ content, and optimize your AI agent's performance based on real rider interactions.
</Tip>
