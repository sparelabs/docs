<Warning>
For **emergency system disruptions**, where a system issue is impacting multiple users or trips, refer to our [Spare Urgent Escalation Procedures](/help-center/general-support/spare-urgent-escalation-procedures).
</Warning>

## Contact Support

You can contact the Support team via two methods:

### Email Support

If you prefer to contact us via email, you can reach us at [support@sparelabs.com](mailto:support@sparelabs.com).

<Tip>
Please provide as much detail as possible about your question or issue so we can assist you efficiently.
</Tip>

### Live Chat Support

To start a live chat with our support team, simply click on the webchat icon located on our website.

This will launch the live chat widget, where you can:
- View help center articles
- Check the status of the platform
- Start a live chat with a support team member

![Contact Support chat widget](/images/general-support/contact-support-chat-widget.png)

<Info>
Our live chat support is available **24/7** and provides real-time assistance. If the live chat is unavailable, you can still send us a message, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
</Info>
