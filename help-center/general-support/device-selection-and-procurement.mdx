Now that you've decided to partner with Spare, it's time to choose the devices for your vehicles! Device selection and installation is crucial to ensure your drivers can operate the Spare Driver app safely and reliably.

## Device Selection Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Device Type">
    Choose the right device to run the Spare Driver app. Consider these factors:

    ### Device Compatibility
    - **Supported platforms**: Both smartphones and tablets
    - **Screen size**: Variable based on vehicle type, but shouldn't impede driver's view
    - **Operating system**: iOS recommended for reliability (Android supported)

    ### Technical Requirements
    - **iOS**: Version 13 or higher
    - **Android**: OS 8 or higher
    - **Hardware**: Strong OEM brands (Apple, Samsung)
    - **Recommended models**: 6th generation+ LTE iPads or iPhone 7s+
  </Step>

  <Step title="Plan Device Installation">
    Determine optimal placement and mounting solutions:

    ### Placement Considerations
    - Accessible and visible to drivers
    - Must not impede road visibility
    - Easy to interact with while driving safely

    <Callout type="info">
      **Recommended Mounting**: We strongly recommend using mounts from [Ram Mounts](https://rammount.com/) for professional installation in appropriate vehicle locations.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Purchase Cellular Plan">
    Set up reliable connectivity for your devices:

    ### Service Requirements
    - Strong, reliable telecommunications provider
    - Excellent coverage in your service area
    - LTE cellular data plans

    ### Data Specifications
    - **Minimum**: 5GB monthly data per device
    - **Usage estimate**: 5GB supports 12 hours/day, 6 days/week operation
    - **Purpose**: Location updates and route data for Spare platform
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ensure Power Supply">
    Maintain device operation throughout service hours:

    - Devices must remain charged during entire duty periods
    - Install vehicle-compatible chargers
    - Consider backup power solutions for extended operations

    <Callout type="warning">
      Device battery failure mid-duty can disrupt service operations. Ensure reliable power supply.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Device Procurement and Installation

Once you've selected your hardware components (devices, mounts, chargers), create an action plan for procurement and installation.

### Pre-Installation Planning

As you await device delivery, plan your installation strategy:

<Accordion title="Installation Planning Checklist">
  - **Who** will install the devices?
  - **When** will installation occur?
  - **What** specialized tools are required?
  - **Where** will devices be positioned in each vehicle type?
  - **How** will power and data connectivity be established?
</Accordion>

### Installation Timeline

<Callout type="info">
  **Recommended Timeline**: Install devices 1-2 weeks before service launch to allow time for testing and issue resolution.
</Callout>

### Testing and Validation

After installation:

1. **Test drive** each vehicle with installed devices
2. **Verify** app functionality and connectivity
3. **Check** power supply and charging systems
4. **Confirm** optimal positioning and visibility

### Additional Components

Consider installing other required components simultaneously:
- Ticketing systems
- Fare boxes
- Communication equipment
- Safety devices

<Callout type="success">
  Once installation and testing are complete, your vehicles are ready to operate with the Spare Driver app!
</Callout>
