Curious about how you can pay your Spare invoice? This guide explains all available payment options and important billing information.

## Finding Payment Instructions

<Info>
**Easy Access:** Each Spare invoice includes a dedicated payment instructions section with comprehensive remittance information and all necessary details for seamless payment processing.
</Info>

### Invoice Delivery & Copies

- **Primary delivery:** Invoices from Spare are delivered directly to your inbox
- **Additional copies:** If you need another copy of your invoice, send a request to [accounting@sparelabs.com](mailto:accounting@sparelabs.com)

### Regional Considerations

<Note>
Payment instructions may vary based on your country and the currency involved. Always refer to your specific invoice for bank details relevant to your transaction.
</Note>

### Credit Card Payment Process

For the most convenient payment experience:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Pay Now Button">
    Find the **Pay Now** button in the invoice email you received
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Secure Portal">
    Click the button to be redirected to a secure payment page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    Input your credit card information in the secure form
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save for Future Use (Optional)">
    Take advantage of the option to save your credit card information for automatic charges on future invoices
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Convenience Feature:** Saving your payment information enables automatic processing of future invoices, reducing administrative overhead for your team.
</Tip>

### Important Payment Requirements

<Warning>
**Fee Responsibility:** Any bank fees or related charges arising from the transaction are the responsibility of the payer and should be settled in full, without deduction from the invoice payment amount.
</Warning>

For additional questions about payments, contact our accounting team at [accounting@sparelabs.com](mailto:accounting@sparelabs.com).

## Accounts Receivable Collection Policy

Understanding Spare's payment terms and collection procedures helps ensure smooth business relationships.

### Standard Payment Terms

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Payment Due" icon="calendar">
    **30 days** from invoice date unless alternative arrangements have been mutually agreed upon
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account Risk" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    **75 days** past due may result in account termination
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
**Flexible Arrangements:** If you need alternative payment terms, contact our accounting team before the invoice due date to discuss mutually agreeable arrangements.
</Note>

### Collection Timeline

The following timeline applies to overdue accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invoice Due (30 Days)">
    Standard payment period expires
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="First Notice (45 Days)">
    Initial payment reminder sent
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final Notice (60 Days)">
    Final payment request before account action
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Account Review (75 Days)">
    Account may be suspended or terminated for non-payment
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Account Protection:** To avoid service interruption, ensure payments are submitted well before the 75-day mark. Contact [accounting@sparelabs.com](mailto:accounting@sparelabs.com) if you anticipate any payment delays.
</Warning>
