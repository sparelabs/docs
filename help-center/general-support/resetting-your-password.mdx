## Resetting Your Own Password

If you've forgotten your password, resetting it on the Spare Platform is straightforward. Follow these simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Login Page">
    Go to [https://platform.sparelabs.com/login](https://platform.sparelabs.com/login)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Forgot Password">
    Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Enter your email address associated with your Spare Platform account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Reset Email">
    Click **Send password reset email**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You will receive an email with a password reset link. Click this link to proceed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    Fill out your new password in the form provided
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive the password reset email within a few minutes.
</Info>

## Resetting a Driver's Password

If you have administrative privileges on the Spare Platform, you can send a password reset email on behalf of drivers.

<Note>
This feature requires administrative access to the Users section of the platform.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Users Section">
    Select **Users** from the sidebar navigation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Drivers">
    In the dropdown menu, select **Drivers**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Driver">
    Find and select the driver for whom you would like to reset the password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Reset Email">
    Under **Driver Details**, click **Send Password Reset Email**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Notify Driver">
    The driver will receive an email with a reset link. Ask them to click this link and create a new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
It's helpful to inform the driver that they should expect a password reset email and check their spam folder if they don't see it immediately.
</Tip>
