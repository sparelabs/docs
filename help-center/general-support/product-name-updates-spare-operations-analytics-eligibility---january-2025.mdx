<Info>
Starting **February 3, 2025**, three products in the **Spare Platform** will have new names. These updates better reflect what they do in your daily work.
</Info>

## What's changing?

<Warning>
Only the names are changing! The new names will appear when you hover over icons and in the top left corner of the platform.
</Warning>

The way you **use the platform, its functionality, and support will stay the same**—no action is needed from you.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Spare Launch → Spare Operations" icon="arrow-right">
    This tool started as a way to launch microtransit, but today it's used to run and manage some of the largest demand-response transit services. The new name reflects its role in daily operations.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Spare Engage → Spare Eligibility" icon="arrow-right">
    This tool is all about managing and simplifying paratransit eligibility. The new name makes it clearer that it helps process applications, track statuses, and keep riders moving.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Spare Analyze → Spare Analytics" icon="arrow-right">
    A small name update to better describe what it does—help you track, report, and gain insights from your transit data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to expect

<Steps>
  <Step title="New names will be visible">
    You'll see the new names in the **Spare Platform starting February 3, 2025**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="No functional changes">
    No changes to how the tools work
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="No action required">
    No action is needed from you
  </Step>
</Steps>
