## How Stripe Works

**What is Stripe and how does it work?**

Stripe is a payment service provider that helps merchants accept diverse payment methods like credit and debit cards. Particularly designed for online businesses, Stripe Payments offers unique features to improve the overall online sales experience.

![Stripe Payment Process](/images/general-support/stripe-payment-process.png)

<Callout type="info">
  For detailed information about how Stripe can help your business, visit the [Stripe Payment Methods Guide](https://stripe.com/guides/payment-methods-guide#3-how-stripe-can-help).
</Callout>

## Available Reports

**What reports can you generate in Stripe?**

Stripe provides comprehensive reporting capabilities to help you track and analyze your payment data:

### Report Types
- **Transaction summaries** - Detailed breakdown of all payments
- **Sales performance** - Revenue analysis and trends
- **Customer insights** - Payment behavior and patterns
- **Financial reconciliation** - Data for accounting purposes

![Stripe Reports Dashboard](/images/general-support/stripe-reports-dashboard.png)

<Callout type="info">
  Learn more about generating reports in the [Stripe Basic Financial Reports documentation](https://stripe.com/docs/reports).
</Callout>

## Bank Account Connection

**How can you connect bank accounts to Stripe?**

Connecting your bank account to Stripe enables seamless fund transfers and automated payouts.

### Connection Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Stripe Dashboard">
    Log in to your Stripe Dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Payouts">
    Go to the "Payouts" section in your dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Bank Account">
    Click "Add Bank Account" and enter your banking details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Account">
    Complete verification process if required
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Receive confirmation that your bank account is connected
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Bank Account Setup](/images/general-support/stripe-bank-account-setup.png)

<Callout type="info">
  For detailed instructions, see [Stripe's guide to adding bank accounts for payouts](https://support.stripe.com/questions/add-a-bank-account-for-payouts).
</Callout>

## Deposit Fund Management

**How do deposit funds work for bank accounts?**

Stripe facilitates automated bank transfers through its payout system:

### How It Works
1. **Request Payouts** - Funds are automatically or manually transferred from your Stripe account
2. **Processing** - Stripe processes the payout request
3. **Bank Transfer** - Funds are transferred to your linked bank account
4. **Notifications** - You receive transaction details via the Stripe Dashboard
5. **Bank Statement** - The transfer appears in your bank statement

![Deposit Process](/images/general-support/stripe-deposit-process.png)

<Callout type="info">
  Learn more about bank transfers in the [Stripe Bank Transfer Payments documentation](https://stripe.com/docs/payments/bank-transfers).
</Callout>

## General Ledger Reconciliation

**How to reconcile Stripe transactions with your general ledger?**

Reconciliation ensures your Stripe records align with your company's accounting system:

### Reconciliation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export Stripe Data">
    Download financial data from your Stripe Dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Import to Accounting Software">
    Import the Stripe data into your accounting system
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Match Transactions">
    Compare and match transactions between both systems
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make Adjustments">
    Resolve any discrepancies and make necessary adjustments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Reports">
    Create accurate financial reports for your company books
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Reconciliation Process](/images/general-support/stripe-reconciliation-process.png)

<Callout type="info">
  For comprehensive reconciliation guidance, visit the [Stripe Reconciliation documentation](https://stripe.com/docs/reconciliation).
</Callout>
