## Getting Started

Once you have set up your Spare Platform account, you can access the Spare Platform Dashboard through your web browser.

### Access URL

Visit **[platform.sparelabs.com](https://platform.sparelabs.com/)** to log into your Spare Platform account.

## Browser Requirements

<Callout type="info">
  **Recommended Browser**: Google Chrome (version 63 or newer) is required for optimal performance.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  Some functionalities may not be available on other browser applications such as Internet Explorer, Safari, or Firefox. For the best experience, please use Google Chrome.
</Callout>
