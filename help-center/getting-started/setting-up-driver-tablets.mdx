To set up your driver tablets and start testing, complete the following steps in order. Each step is essential for proper functionality.

<Info>
Make sure you have administrative access to the Spare platform before beginning this setup process.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before setting up driver tablets, ensure you have completed the following administrative tasks:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Spare Driver App">
    Download the Spare Driver app for iOS or Android by searching for **"spare driver"** in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="iOS Download" icon="apple">
        Search "spare driver" in the Apple App Store
      </Card>
      <Card title="Android Download" icon="google-play">
        Search "spare driver" in the Google Play Store
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Driver Accounts">
    Ensure you have set up driver accounts on the [administrator portal](https://platform.sparelabs.com/).
    
    <Note>
      If you have not set up a driver account yet, follow the detailed steps in our [Setting Up Driver Accounts](https://help.sparelabs.com/en/articles/8969226-setting-up-driver-accounts) guide.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Service and Vehicles">
    Ensure you have created a service, added vehicles, and created duties on the administrator portal.
    
    <Warning>
      This step is crucial for the driver app to function properly. Without proper service configuration, drivers will not receive trip assignments.
    </Warning>
    
    If you have not completed this setup, follow the comprehensive steps in our [Service General Setup](https://help.sparelabs.com/en/articles/8988085-service-general-setup) guide, which covers:
    - Creating a service
    - Adding vehicles to your fleet
    - Creating duties and schedules
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Setup Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure all components are properly configured:

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="App Installation">
    - [ ] Spare Driver app downloaded from official app store
    - [ ] App installed on driver tablets
    - [ ] App opens without errors
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="Account Configuration">
    - [ ] Driver accounts created in administrator portal
    - [ ] Driver login credentials distributed
    - [ ] Drivers can successfully log into the app
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="Service Setup">
    - [ ] Service created and configured
    - [ ] Vehicles added to the fleet
    - [ ] Duties and schedules created
    - [ ] Test trips can be dispatched
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## Next Steps

Once you have completed the setup:

1. **Test the configuration** by creating a test trip and ensuring it appears on the driver tablet
2. **Train your drivers** on how to use the app effectively
3. **Monitor the system** during initial deployment to identify any issues

<Tip>
Consider setting up a few test accounts and vehicles first to validate your configuration before rolling out to your entire fleet.
</Tip>
