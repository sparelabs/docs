title: "How to Change the Screen Color"
description: "Learn how to adjust screen color settings in the Spare Driver app with automatic, light, and dark mode options"
---

The Spare Driver app offers three screen color options to customize your viewing experience: **Automatic**, **Light Mode**, and **Dark Mode**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Open the Spare Driver app and tap on the gear icon in the top left corner.
    
    ![Spare Driver app home screen showing settings gear icon in top left corner](/images/spare-driver/screen-color-change-step-1.jpeg)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Screen Color Settings">
    Go to Settings to access the screen color options.
    
    ![Settings menu in the Spare Driver app](/images/spare-driver/screen-color-change-step-2.jpeg)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Preferred Mode">
    Select from the available screen color options:
    
    ![Screen color selection options showing Automatic, Light Mode, and Dark Mode](/images/spare-driver/screen-color-change-step-3.jpeg)
    
    ![Example of dark mode interface in the Spare Driver app](/images/spare-driver/screen-color-change-step-4.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Screen Color Options

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Automatic" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    The screen automatically switches to **Light Mode** during the day and **Dark Mode** at night based on your device's time settings.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Light Mode" icon="sun">
    A bright interface ideal for daytime use and well-lit environments.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Dark Mode" icon="moon">
    A darker interface that reduces eye strain in low-light conditions and saves battery on OLED displays.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
The automatic mode is recommended as it adapts to your environment throughout the day, providing optimal visibility for both daytime and nighttime driving.
</Tip>
