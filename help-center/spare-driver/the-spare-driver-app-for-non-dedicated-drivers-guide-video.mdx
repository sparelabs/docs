<Info>
This guide is designed for **non-dedicated drivers** (independent contractors, TNC drivers, and taxi drivers) who choose their own hours and select which trips to accept. For dedicated drivers with assigned shifts, please see the [Dedicated Driver Guide](/help-center/spare-driver/the-spare-driver-app-for-dedicated-drivers).
</Info>

## Overview

As a non-dedicated driver, you have the flexibility to:
- Choose your own working hours
- Select which trips to accept
- Work on-demand based on your availability
- Manage your own schedule through the app

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and Install">
    Install the Spare Driver app from your device's app store. See our [installation guide](/help-center/spare-driver/spare-driver-application-device-installation) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Complete the driver registration process and account setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Guide">
    Download and review the PDF guide to understand all app features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Driving">
    Go online in the app and start accepting trips when you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
