## Overview

The Spare Driver app includes an optional **Driver-to-Rider calling** feature that enables secure communication between drivers and passengers. This feature uses anonymous VOIP technology to protect privacy for both parties.

<Info>
This feature is **especially helpful** when drivers need to locate riders or coordinate pickup details.
</Info>

  <Card title="Privacy Protection" icon="shield">

<Warning>
This is a **premium add-on feature** that incurs additional carrier phone fees. Contact your Spare representative to enable this functionality.
</Warning>

## How to Use Driver-to-Rider Calling

Follow these simple steps to call a rider using the anonymous calling feature:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Rider Information">
    Locate the rider you need to call on your manifest and tap the **Info button** next to their name.
    
    ![Driver app interface showing the Info button next to a rider's name in the manifest](/images/spare-driver/driver-rider-calling-step-1.jpeg)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate the Call">
    In the rider information screen, tap the **Call icon** (green phone button) to start the call.
    
    <Tip>
    Make sure your device volume is turned up before initiating the call.
    </Tip>
    
    ![Call interface showing the green phone icon button to initiate a call to the rider](/images/spare-driver/driver-rider-calling-step-2.jpeg)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="End the Call">
    When you're finished with the conversation, tap the **red phone icon** to end the call. Then tap the **X** in the top left corner to return to your manifest.
    
    ![Call interface showing the red phone icon to end the call and X button to return to the manifest](/images/spare-driver/driver-rider-calling-step-3.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Use Cases

The Driver-to-Rider calling feature is particularly useful for:

- **Location Clarification:** When a rider's pickup location is unclear or hard to find
- **Arrival Notifications:** Letting riders know you've arrived at their location
- **Schedule Updates:** Communicating any delays or changes to pickup times
- **Accessibility Coordination:** Arranging special assistance or accommodation needs

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Call Won't Connect">
    - Verify you have cellular or data connectivity
    - Check that the calling feature is enabled for your account
    - Ensure the rider has provided a valid phone number
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor Call Quality">
    - Move to an area with better cellular reception
    - Check your device's microphone and speaker functionality
    - Restart the app if call quality issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Feature Not Available">
    - Contact your fleet administrator to confirm the feature is enabled
    - Verify your driver account has calling permissions
    - Speak with your Spare representative about adding this feature
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
