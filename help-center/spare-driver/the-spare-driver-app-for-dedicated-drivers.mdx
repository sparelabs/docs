This guide is designed for **dedicated drivers** (employee drivers with assigned shifts and specific routes) who do not choose their own trips. For non-dedicated drivers, please see the [Non-Dedicated Driver Guide](/help-center/spare-driver/the-spare-driver-app-for-non-dedicated-drivers-guide-video).
</Info>

## Quick Start Guide

The Spare Driver App streamlines the experience for dedicated drivers by providing:
- Pre-assigned shifts and routes
- Automatic trip assignments
- Clear passenger manifests
- Real-time navigation support
