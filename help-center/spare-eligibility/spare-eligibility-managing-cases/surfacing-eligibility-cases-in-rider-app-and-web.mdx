## About This Feature

The Eligibility Case Status feature in the Spare Rider app allows riders to check the status of their cases directly within the app, without needing to contact agency staff.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Increased Transparency" icon="eye">
    This increases transparency for riders by providing direct access to their case information.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reduced Workload" icon="chart-line-down">
    Reduces workload and operational costs for you by minimizing the need for phone-based inquiries.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Does It Work?

### Accessing Case Status

**Navigation to Cases**: Riders can view their case statuses by tapping the **Cases** tab in the Spare Rider app. This tab lists all ongoing applications along with their current status.

### Editing Case Visibility

<Steps>
  <Step title="Admin Setup">
    As an admin, navigate to **Eligibility** and select a case type (e.g., Paratransit eligibility application) that you wish to make visible to riders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Case Template">
    Click on **Edit Case Template**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable External Visibility">
    Check the **External Visibility** box to make the case visible to riders. Optionally, provide an external facing name to make it more recognizable for the riders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the changes. The selected cases will now be visible to riders in their app under the **Cases** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Can riders edit their case details in the Rider app?">
  No, riders can view their case status and details but cannot edit them. All edits must be done by agency staff through the Eligibility admin interface.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What types of cases can be made visible to riders?">
  Any case managed within the Eligibility platform can be made visible to riders, as long as the External Visibility option is enabled by an admin.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How are updates communicated to the rider?">
  Updates are automatically reflected in the Rider app under the Cases tab whenever changes are made in the Eligibility admin interface.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can we limit the visibility of certain stages of the case?">
  Yes, admins can choose which stages of the case are visible externally. This ensures that only relevant information is shared with the rider, maintaining confidentiality and clarity in communication.
</Accordion>

  This feature gives riders the ability to check the status of their case directly in the Rider app, improving transparency and reducing support inquiries.
</Callout>
