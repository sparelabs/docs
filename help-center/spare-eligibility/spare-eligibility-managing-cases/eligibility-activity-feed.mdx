## About This Feature

<Note>
This feature was released on **Wednesday, December 18, 2024** after being tested with select customers.
</Note>

![Eligibility Activity Feed interface showing activity timeline and comment section](/images/spare-eligibility/managing-cases/activity-feed-interface.png)

The Activity Feed for Spare Eligibility simplifies case management by consolidating all actions that have taken place on a case into a single, intuitive Activity tab. This update allows users to quickly audit case activities and also add comments for seamless collaboration.

## How It Works

### Accessing the Activity Tab

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Case">
    Open Spare Eligibility and select a case
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Activity Tab">
    Click on the **Activity** tab to view a comprehensive log of actions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### View Case History

The Activity tab displays all tracked activities including:
- Case creation
- Status changes
- Document uploads
- Letters generated
- Form submissions and updates

<Tip>
Every activity is tracked with a timestamp for complete clarity and audit purposes.
</Tip>

## Adding Comments to a Case

### Creating Comments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Comment Field">
    Click on the comment field within the Activity tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag Team Members">
    Use the `@` symbol to tag team members directly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post Your Comment">
    Type your message and hit **Post** to save your comment
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Notifications

<Info>
Tagged users will receive alerts in the admin interface, ensuring timely collaboration. Users can opt-out from receiving these notifications in their personal settings.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is this different from the old Activity Log?">
    The new Activity tab consolidates all case updates into a single view and provides more detailed information on what was changed, including timestamps without having to click to view more information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit or delete comments?">
    At this time, comments cannot be edited or deleted to maintain an accurate audit trail.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who gets notified when a comment is added?">
    Notifications are sent to any team member tagged in the comment using the `@` symbol. Members can turn off these notifications in their personal settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What types of actions are logged?">
    All key actions, such as form submissions or updates, status updates, group memberships, and letter generation are recorded along with their timestamps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
