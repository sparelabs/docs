## About this Feature

The Custom Data Tables feature in Spare Eligibility allows users to view and organize data fields in a way that best fits their specific needs. It provides an at-a-glance view of essential information, streamlining the data management experience for different roles, such as operations managers and booking agents, who need quick access to custom views.

<Info>
  Custom data tables are personalized per user, meaning your customizations won't affect other team members' views.
</Info>

## How Does It Work?

### Accessing Custom Data Tables

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Spare Eligibility">
    Start by logging into the Spare Eligibility platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Data Table Icon">
    Click on the "two-window" icon to open the settings for customizing your data table view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Customizing Table Fields

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Select Fields to Display" icon="eye">
    Click on any available field in the settings menu to add it to your view. This allows you to show additional information that may not be displayed by default.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reorder Fields" icon="arrows-up-down">
    Drag and drop fields to change the order in which they are displayed on the table. This setup helps you arrange data by priority or frequency of use.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Saving Custom Views

Changes made to the data table view are saved to your individual profile. This means:

- **Personalized View**: Each user in your organization can set up their view independently, which does not impact others.
- **Consistent Access**: When you log back in, your custom setup remains in place, ensuring a consistent experience every time.

<Tip>
  Organize your fields by priority or frequency of use to maximize efficiency in your daily workflow.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I reset the custom table view to the default settings?">
    Yes, you can revert to the default settings by selecting the "Reset" option in the data table settings menu.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will other users see the changes I make to the data table?">
    No, customizations are saved individually per user. Each person in your organization can create a personalized view without affecting others.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are there any limitations on the fields I can add to the custom table?">
    While you can add most fields related to your user role in Spare Eligibility, some system-restricted fields might not be available for customization. Please consult your Spare Representative for specific limitations based on your access level.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Some system-restricted fields may not be available for customization depending on your user role and access level.
</Warning>
