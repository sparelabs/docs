---
title: "Generating and Editing Case Letter Templates"
description: "Learn how to create and edit case letter templates in Spare Eligibility for personalized communication with applicants."
---

Case letter templates are generalized letters you can create in Spare's template builder. Once generated, the template letters can be easily applied to a specific case, auto-populating the user's data for a personalized approach.

## Generate a Case Letter Template

<Steps>
<Step title="Access Letter Templates Section">
Scroll down to the **Letter Templates section** on the **Case Template information page**.
</Step>

<Step title="Add New Template">
Click **"+ Add Letter Template"** to create a new template.</Step>

![Case template creation interface](/images/spare-eligibility/managing-case-templates/letter-template-creation.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Format Your Letter">
Use the **formatting toolbar [1]** in the **"Content"** section to stylize your letter. An **automatic preview [2]** will appear on the right.

<Tip>
Click on the **hyperlinks [3]** in the info section to access the stylizing and HTML formatting guides.
</Tip>

![Letter template editor with formatting options](/images/spare-eligibility/managing-case-templates/letter-template-editor.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Save Changes">
Click **"Save"** to apply the changes and create your template.
</Step>
</Steps>

## View and Edit Case Letter Templates

### Viewing Existing Templates

To view existing case letter templates:

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Templates">
Scroll down to the **Letter Templates section** on the **Case Template information page**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Template">
Click on the **letter template name** to see more details.

![Letter templates list view](/images/spare-eligibility/managing-case-templates/letter-templates-list.png)
</Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Templates

To edit an existing letter template:

1. Navigate to the template details page (following the steps above)
2. Click **"Edit Letter Template"** on the Letter Template Details page

![Edit letter template button location](/images/spare-eligibility/managing-case-templates/edit-letter-template-button.png)

<Note>
Once you click "Edit Letter Template", you'll be taken to the same formatting interface used when creating new templates, where you can make your desired changes and save them.
</Note>
