# Managing Cases with Spare Eligibility

To create and manage your cases effectively, you'll need to utilize different elements of Spare Eligibility. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the eligibility case creation and management process.

<Note>
This article covers the high-level workflow for managing cases in Spare Eligibility. Each step links to detailed guides for implementation.
</Note>

### Generating an All Cases Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Download">
    Click on the **Download** icon to begin the report generation process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Parameters">
    **Set the time range** for your report and **choose the file format** that meets your needs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Report">
    Click **"Download Report"** to generate and download your comprehensive cases report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![All Cases report download interface](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964448786/147a648015a915f04fd3384b/6LoNTchQ2r43OlRFtENUl3LL_zt5f6wJnw.png?expires=1753820100&signature=091cc94539c8e6960d256109b1dd19d26d27de45a0649ec9d9fa8b650bfef6dc&req=fSYjEs12molZFb4f3HP0gGBFO87Nob%2BjwSvTkIj58Uo2MbgKHruCi7%2FL0gIG%0Aj8o%3D%0A)

<Caption>
Report download interface showing time range selection and file format options.
</Caption>

### Building a Custom Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Report">
    Click **"+ Create New Report"** to start building your custom analytics report.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Metrics">
    Choose the specific metrics (columns) you'd like to include in your custom report.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Report">
    **Name** your report and click **"Save"** to store your custom configuration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Custom Report">
    Once the report appears on the dashboard, click **"Download"** to set the time range and file format, then download your custom report.
  </Step>
</Steps>
