# NTD Reporting

National Transit Database (NTD) reporting is a critical requirement for transit agencies receiving federal funding. This guide provides access to comprehensive NTD reporting resources and documentation.

<Info>
The NTD is the Federal Transit Administration's (FTA) primary national database for statistics on the transit systems of the United States.
</Info>
