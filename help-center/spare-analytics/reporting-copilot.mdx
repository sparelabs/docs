# Reporting Copilot

Copilot is designed to help you speed up common workflows. Reporting Copilot specifically helps data analysts find and create custom reports easily using AI-powered assistance.

<Info>
Reporting Copilot is available as part of the **Data Analyst Pro Package**. Contact your Account Manager if you need this feature enabled for your organization.
</Info>

## How It Works

### Step 1: Access the Feature

Navigate to **Spare Analytics** → **Reporting** → **Create New Report**

  src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1242389550/df4f743317cc331e09893c48289a/Screenshot+2024-11-06+at+1_03_05%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1753820100&signature=743695facc5c6e25837be953de0357dcc277bd1eb3e3eb4b0bfcc2fe0a3f2c60&req=dSIjFMp2lIRaWfMW1HO4zVo5EGI56Zst6nhJv7wEO3GcdQyNR1jmJZ7cP9s6%0A5X66%0A" 
  alt="Screenshot showing the Reporting Copilot interface in Spare Analytics"
/>

### Step 2: Describe Your Needs

Describe to Copilot what kind of report you're looking for in natural language.

<img 
  src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1242389825/f9d5821a17169d563dfec67e7edf/Screenshot+2024-11-06+at+1_03_22%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1753820100&signature=e43c47279210e397e1bacb05023cbc29eeedbea29f143de04f69a239ad61096e&req=dSIjFMp2lIldXPMW1HO4zRXwRMss3c6fB1bB1LE9uGEkz2GiTU%2Fv8WyEyWu1%0ASMq3%0A" 
  alt="Screenshot showing how to describe report requirements to Copilot"
/>

### Step 3: Let Copilot Work

Click **"Ask Copilot"** to let the AI analyze your request.

<img 
  src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1242389973/6797a6752eae304ad9d4d4c3e135/Screenshot+2024-11-06+at+1_03_26%E2%80%AFPM+%282%29.png?expires=1753820100&signature=a0deb256aed0b523c7278ab0f5a1ac2c88b28af497f1b07067a624b2d94a47fc&req=dSIjFMp2lIhYWvMW1HO4zbyt0e62wHyPpWW09x%2F2iuEjcx%2FgtWmZb405i5ke%0Afk7D%0A" 
  alt="Screenshot showing the Ask Copilot button"
/>

### Step 4: Review Auto-Selected Metrics

Copilot will automatically select the data you need to create your custom report based on your description.

<img 
  src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1242390379/30642d7c5d41f39fb3eb0ae51ec1/Screenshot+2024-11-06+at+1_03_34%E2%80%AFPM+%282%29.png?expires=1753820100&signature=d70df0f67944ffedd7cbaf7fe3dc30610300dffe28e9f69c1759f712a7d3d9a9&req=dSIjFMp3nYJYUPMW1HO4zYa6h4m7JaWv8Eje2Rcc3M5yZ1e%2FgZByJKImPGq9%0An5md%0A" 
  alt="Screenshot showing Copilot's automatically selected metrics for the custom report"
/>

### Step 5: Save Your Report

Copilot will generate a suggested name and description for your report. Review and save when ready.

<img 
  src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1242390648/b8e4e3eed1b1bd6ed9a5135ebe9d/Screenshot+2024-11-06+at+1_03_56%E2%80%AFPM+%282%29.png?expires=1753820100&signature=7c8a77d0089cba80e888ac988da730f381049c6c63f2f0133aab9a8821cc5987&req=dSIjFMp3nYdbUfMW1HO4zdERcpLGhtLIwuAVghvraOK2wajFckdU%2B2AHl0UM%0AoeuE%0A" 
  alt="Screenshot showing the final step of saving a custom report with Copilot-generated name and description"
/>

<Success>
Your custom report is now saved and accessible anytime from your **Custom Reports** section!
</Success>
