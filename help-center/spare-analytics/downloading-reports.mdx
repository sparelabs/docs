# Downloading Reports

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about accessing and downloading reports from Spare Analytics.

<Info>
Before you begin, ensure you have **platform access** and either the **Admin** or **Access to Reporting** role in your organization.
</Info>

<Warning>
If you do not have the required permission roles, please contact your account administrator for access.
</Warning>

## Quick Reference

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How to access Spare Analytics reports?" icon="question">
    Navigate to platform.sparelabs.com, then click on **Spare Analytics** in the left sidebar, followed by **Reporting** in the bottom left.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What are the types of Spare reports?" icon="list">
    There are two main types: **Default Reports** (pre-built Ridership, NTD, and Duty reports) and **Custom Reports** (built by you with specific metrics).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How to download a pre-built report?" icon="download">
    Select the report type, click **Download**, set parameters (date range, fleet/service), choose file format, and click **Download Report**.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How to build a custom report?" icon="wrench">
    Click **+ Create New Report**, choose metrics (Ridership or Duty), select specific metrics, name and save, then download or save to platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Download Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Report Type">
    Choose the specific report type you want to download from the available options.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Download">
    Click the **Download** button next to your selected report.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Parameters">
    Set the following parameters for your report:
    - **Date range**: Choose your desired time period
    - **Days of the week**: Select specific days if needed
    - **Fleet(s) or service(s)**: Choose which fleets or services to include
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose File Format">
    Select either **CSV** or **XLSX** file format (both open in Excel and Google Sheets).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Report">
    Click **Download Report** to generate and download your file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Choose Your Action

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create New Report" icon="floppy-disk">
    **Saves the report** in the Custom Reports section for future editing and downloading. Choose this if you'll use the report regularly.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Download Report" icon="download">
    **Generates the report** in CSV or XLSX format but doesn't save it to the platform. Choose this for one-time reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
The "Create New Report" option allows you to edit and download the report later, while the "Download Report" option is for immediate, one-time use.
</Note>

## Managing Custom Reports

### Editing Existing Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Report">
    Find your custom report in the **Custom Reports** section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Actions">
    Click the **Actions** button next to the report name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Edit Option">
    You can edit:
    - Report columns (metrics)
    - Report name
    - Report description
    - Or delete the report entirely
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downloading Custom Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Report">
    Click **Download** next to your custom report.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Parameters">
    A pop-up menu will appear. Configure:
    - **Date range**: Select your desired time period
    - **Days of the week**: Choose specific days if applicable
    - **Fleet(s)/service(s)**: Select which fleets or services to include
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose File Format">
    Select either **CSV** or **XLSX** format.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download">
    Click **Download Report** to retrieve your file.
  </Step>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't access the reporting section" icon="lock">
    **Solution**: Ensure you have the correct permissions. You need either Admin or Access to Reporting role. Contact your account administrator if you don't have access.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="My download is taking a long time" icon="clock">
    **Solution**: Large date ranges or complex reports may take longer to generate. Try reducing the date range or selecting fewer metrics for faster processing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="The downloaded file won't open" icon="file-excel">
    **Solution**: Ensure you have Excel, Google Sheets, or another compatible spreadsheet application installed. Both CSV and XLSX formats should open in most spreadsheet programs.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I can't find my custom report" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Solution**: Check that you clicked "Create New Report" rather than "Download Report" when you originally created it. Only saved reports appear in the Custom Reports section.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Your downloaded reports will be saved in your browser's default downloads folder. Look for files with names that include the report type and date range you selected.
</Tip>
