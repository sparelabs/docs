# One-click NTD Report

We've launched a new one-click report designed to streamline your NTD S-10 reporting process. This comprehensive report provides all the specific information you need from Spare to complete your submissions quickly and accurately.

<Info>
This report is specifically designed to meet National Transit Database (NTD) S-10 reporting requirements with pre-calculated metrics and proper data grouping.
</Info>

## What's New

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Monthly Averages" icon="calendar-days">
    Pre-calculated monthly averages for all required NTD data points
  </Card>
  <Card title="Annual Reporting" icon="chart-line">
    Comprehensive annual averages for yearly reporting requirements
  </Card>
  <Card title="NTD Compliance" icon="check-circle">
    Data automatically grouped by weekday, Saturday, and Sunday
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
While the report provides the standard weekday/Saturday/Sunday grouping, you can still download the data by daily breakdown. Your current **Fleets** and **Services** reports also remain available.
</Note>

## Data Included

The NTD Report includes all essential metrics required for your S-10 submissions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Vehicle Operations Metrics">
    - Max Active Vehicles
    - Vehicles in Operation
    - Total Actual Vehicle Distance
    - Total Actual Vehicle Revenue Distance
    - Total Scheduled Vehicle Revenue Distance
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Service Hours Metrics">
    - Total Actual Vehicle Hours
    - Total Actual Vehicle Revenue Hours
    - Deadhead Hours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ridership Metrics">
    - Passenger Distance Traveled
    - Unlinked Passenger Trips
    - Deadhead Distance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Access the Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Analytics">
    Click on the **Analytics** section in your sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Reporting">
    Click on **Reporting** from the Analytics menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find NTD Report">
    Look for the **NTD Report** alongside your other operations reports
  </Step>
</Steps>
