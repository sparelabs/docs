# Spare Automated KPI Reports

Stay informed about your service performance with automated KPI reports delivered straight to your inbox. Spare Platform can automatically generate and email comprehensive analytics reports to keep you updated on key metrics without manual effort.

## Daily Reports

In addition to weekly reports, you can enable **daily ridership reports** that focus specifically on passenger and trip data for more frequent monitoring.

## Setting Up Email Reports

Follow these steps to configure automated reports for your organization:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organization Settings">
    Click on your **Organization icon** and select **Settings**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Notifications">
    Go to the **Notifications tab**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Email Reports Section">
    Scroll down to the **Email Reports** section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Report Type">
    Use the **dropdown menu** to choose between weekly KPI reports or daily ridership reports
  </Step>
</Steps>
