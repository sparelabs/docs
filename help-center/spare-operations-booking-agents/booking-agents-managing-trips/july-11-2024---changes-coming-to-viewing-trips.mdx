These changes will be live in the Spare Platform as of July 11th, 2024. 

We know that finding trip details quickly is essential to your ability to react to rider needs! That's why we're releasing an update so you can view individual trip details on the Live Map, as well as more easily find information on the individual trip page.

## New Trip Page

This new page cleanly organizes trip information into tabs:

On the **Overview Page** you'll find information about the timeline of the trip, trip (request) details, duty details, and how it was booked. 

On the **Payments Page **you'll find information about how the trip was paid for, any charges, receipts, and discount information. 

On the **Activity Log Page** you'll find any historical information about the trip including status changes, driver offers, flexibility overrides, and reviews. 

You can **see the vehicle directly from this page!**

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1107167161/1dcb3f959f5075b55f0d03da/Screenshot+2024-07-08+at+9_46_36%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1753820100&signature=c502d4402abd71d25f8aa9773d910103e99297d7ad719b080c733b1685091cb3&req=dSEnEch4moBZWPMW1HO4zdc2kdXwSXIw5jQYRVhamCOk1TmDGyQ5KHF8xA8U%0AXDDJ%0A)The Timeline Details section has also been reorganized.
