## What is Activity?![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/wyxobwoi/1355802866/541d66fdede89a5d507907b54642/Screenshot+2025-01-29+at+1_49_54%E2%80%AFPM.png?expires=1753820100&signature=39f2a18ff4e1234f6332230eb2e1f704b0e0974bd34913a0825837d9c54ea5ab&req=dSMiE8F%2Bn4lZX%2FMW1HO4zRj5azM05G3n%2FuSgqWcyX6f8aSiRUwIcjB7cq5kH%0AMnJ3%0A)The Activity view is your tool for figuring out what happened & when. It can be found all across Spare - for riders, trips, duties, drivers, and more. 

It's a helpful investigation tool for your agency. 

But we've heard feedback that it can be hard to dive into the specifics - there is lots of clicking required to understand exactly what happened.
