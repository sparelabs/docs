---
title: "Deleting Rider Accounts"
description: "Learn how to delete and archive rider accounts in Spare Operations platform"
---

If a rider would like their account to be deleted, they will need to:

- Call or email their respective transit agencies' customer service team and have a representative there delete their account
- Send a note to the Spare team via email at [support@sparelabs.com](mailto:support@sparelabs.com)
- Delete their account using the rider app 

## Archiving Riders in Spare Operations

To remove a rider from the Spare Operations platform, simply head to their page and click on **Actions** in the top right corner. Then click on **Archive Rider**.

As for the Rider App and Rider Web, users who choose to delete their accounts from these self-serve tools will become archived and then their accounts will be automatically deleted within 30 days. This is to ensure compliance with regulatory standards like GDPR.

### Deleting Account via Rider App

If a rider would like to delete their account using the rider app, they will need to:

1. Click on the **Profile Icon**

![Screenshot of the rider app main page showing the profile icon](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1186463894/c6e83e842424589ed41b5664/Main+page.jpg?expires=1753820100&signature=2783653afd85d2b7cb164b330eba1fb08529179b78f1328787fb89c42b387108&req=dSEvEM14nolWXfMW1HO4zafxTjjZXB4Srh%2BzVcvg%2BetXQbY2Ls%2FCLCW5D7Mt%0AGIHZ%0A)

2. Scroll to the bottom and click on **Delete account**
