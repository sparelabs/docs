---
title: "Enabling Walking Directions"
description: "Learn how to enable realistic walking directions for your service to provide riders with turn-by-turn routes to their pick-up and drop-off destinations."
---

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972917651/9deb863c710420c6060b8cc3/MyRACvei_vkTti69ev4OmftFXe6x2N9fzA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=71266d0a6e251e57a7a2191f3011c880867fc50f12dd787de95332378916796f&req=fSclH8h5m4ReFb4f3HP0gKbp4YkvTJ%2B7fdEuM7NGqIE9hXhlep18k3hbkURD%0AkZ4%3D%0A)Walking directions show riders a real walking route to get to and from their pick-up or drop-off destinations.

If you have Administrator access, you can enable walking directions for your service:

1. Go to the **Service(s)** you would like walking directions enabled for
2. Click **"Edit Service"**

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972917658/5683f2492fb4d1817dbaa0c5/XXG3aPKh8P2mEcrA6mh3FcB4Ln5Jf4ytlg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=9f0f3c0222e46fd6447c5f61217deb20a1fa8a26e616cb936e75ea738a15d35f&req=fSclH8h5m4RXFb4f3HP0gJvbhj2ypNpnouYaz0QYQsAVe%2BcRpDBvlim03NKU%0ABhI%3D%0A)

3. Scroll to the bottom and select **"Walking Directions"**
4. Select **"Realistic Route"**

**Note:** Walking Directions will be set to the default of "Straight Line". Straight Line directions will not provide riders with a turn-by-turn route to get to where they need to go, unlike the Realistic Route setting that does. See the images below for examples of both.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972917668/48cbfa758186e9d51e11113b/S76Ed37JQi5eqC_cz8snRUpPMpXDtJTa3w.png?expires=1753820100&signature=6d3ba78d139befad47c9e20631a904ae0362cee9d13bb323206c9d4da548a0b3&req=fSclH8h5m4dXFb4f3HP0gOM7nhwVc1RYFF0D%2F%2FXOyatBMKin8uYd1FCbiqW4%0ALII%3D%0A)

5. **Save** All Changes

Now riders will be able to see a **turn-by-turn route** to walk to where they need to go for pick-ups or drop-offs.

An example of **"Straight Line" (default) versus "Realistic Route" **walking directions.
