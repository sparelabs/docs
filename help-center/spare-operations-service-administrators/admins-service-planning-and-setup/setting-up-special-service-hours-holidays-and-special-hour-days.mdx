---
title: "Setting up Special Service Hours (Holidays and Special-Hour Days)"
description: "Adjusting service operating hours can be done by adding Special Hours to a Time Rule."
---

While the Time Rule on a Zone sets the standard operating hours, Special Hours allow you to create days and times where your service has no or modified hour

For more information on creating time rules, check the article below.

[Time Rules](https://help.sparelabs.com/en/articles/8994601-time-rules)

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706533/9fd923430039f42ebb3d32cb/TIT47UViAwmxGWTULL6Ux7eh5_2aMNKabg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=f207f6b952a510267ac093dc482a1ef4a89f1b551e6ce4465e882e05f2ccf110&req=fSclEcl4mIJcFb4f3HP0gBF5vbp3sMQ70fSVD9QHAkmHyGuSWQsR9%2FrPqoVx%0Al0w%3D%0A)

To access Time Rules, use the left side menu to select **Service Planning** and then choose **Time Rules** from the dropdown.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706536/16f963ec7130e782b94098ca/OLd_zR9Aw4OwBJ3Y3a5BdAXsMtMusGqlRg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=eca25b1e47dd3c1e1a2f7d5d403cfef8dce51bf3dc87f79dc3222189539c7800&req=fSclEcl4mIJZFb4f3HP0gG%2BNb%2BHEb7qu5fQ6O4VFg6Vd3emuQLSn9YmZBEVn%0AM2c%3D%0A)

Search for and click on the **Time Rule that you would like to update**. Select **Edit Time Rule** to add the Special Hours.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706543/7173c32f8789b0b73dcf0c4e/2s0kXFtDRcK-v74kU4_M0ULNzrKXLIZ4Gg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=38a187f32369d2796957d2a1b03957c32bae7aac45d0b87096b3b4583d33ff28&req=fSclEcl4mIVcFb4f3HP0gAhaZ%2B%2Fqn51pwOvQ3dONcJCn4T7oBFcw4X4XkC6%2F%0Af44%3D%0A)

You can add Special Hours (i.e., exclusion dates/hours) by clicking Add.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706548/f63b6171d7c8bd1acf01d596/ufVrKryTp_2NOlLBLVoDMu2Zc_cuxScvrg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=52d89ba7837c5a05d99d7a2b8889c9f5e13ba6ee6ae3c7348cf03822195b5ad6&req=fSclEcl4mIVXFb4f3HP0gD%2FWo3nnq7EDOR5iknxYZl9iJwQ8Ydm3R7ozlSyT%0A3Bc%3D%0A)

Once you click Add, the **“Add Special Hours”** window will open. You can then choose to turn your service off for the specified day or modify the hours.

**No Service Day:** You can simply click “Done” to exclude the day selected.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706552/3fea4068571d180fbeee8b3c/c-B8FUf9YEyCvwzpqC3NYur4hJ1sv-KgYw.png?expires=1753820100&signature=c508bb6c62620c84cd9a6263315ababf2f74582fdd8ad0925b93d8447b6bcb3f&req=fSclEcl4mIRdFb4f3HP0gMF90AQCx%2FVq76vjcmGF%2FmrZxovrfuOdHO6l2Mj6%0ARdo%3D%0A)

**Modified Hours:** Unselect “Exclusion Date” if you would like **to add modified hours instead **of a no-service day.

**To modify operating hours**, choose the date and updated service hours, then select “Done”.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972706555/d728cb8e893602a728aef8d6/BJUnz6x2qE_W4Oc0xGzsCjkdlY4DGkylQA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=a027529fce70fc8cf3fc30f05b4bc3b233648e9b793352ead3d9fef6aac78515&req=fSclEcl4mIRaFb4f3HP0gNoobb1reUhOpAhgQ2DgWLM3FVeH1S%2BA3RwwyZqv%0Ajeg%3D%0A)

The last step is to select the** "Save" **button on the bottom right-hand side of the screen. This will save your progress and apply your updates.
