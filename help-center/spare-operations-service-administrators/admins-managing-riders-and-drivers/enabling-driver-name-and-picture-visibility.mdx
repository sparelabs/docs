---
title: "Enabling Driver Name and Picture Visibility"
description: ""
---

Driver name and picture visibility are available with Rider App version 2.23.72 or later.

With driver name and picture visibility, riders can see more information about their driver when they book a trip through the Rider App. This information includes:

Driver** first name**;

Driver **profile image**;

**The vehicle color, make, and model** identification;

Vehicle** license plate**;

To enable driver visibility, please follow these steps:

**Note: Only system administrators will be able to make this change.**

- Log in to the Spare Platform.

Click **"Service Planning" **then **select the Service(s) **you would like to enable Driver Profile visibility for:

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972667616/549c398a0d5b9541933dfad3/TSF-KlMQn0TfKYTTCUZ-8xVVYeGotWfoMQ.png?expires=1753820100&signature=15d14c37e3cb9eb3e48d65b8a56ac74d8815b8dd63c46f59a349b6279541d0c5&req=fSclEM95m4BZFb4f3HP0gKAGI4apgEr1OdIsXYNQ3YnEMI%2F7Pi%2BDvGOD77di%0AwV4%3D%0A)
