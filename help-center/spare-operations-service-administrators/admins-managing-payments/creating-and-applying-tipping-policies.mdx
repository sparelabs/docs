You can change the tipping rate as you like and apply the tipping rate to services. This will enable you to set a different tipping rate for each service and keep drivers happy!

## Access Tip Policies

Under **Spare Pay**, go to **Tip Policies** and click on **+ Add Tip Policy**.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972640441/4e076d9dd74ad8f418092a57/Nvm99fTd4e7lTB0GBb49qdkazRADwkUR0Q.png?expires=1753820100&signature=b1d2d1a6ce7cf66b6823a825b394c585d2491531f7dba7d1e65089f9bfaff497&req=fSclEM1%2BmYVeFb4f3HP0gCigjCQ4A81%2BcAKrF3UAN7GMXWjvz2CqH3mHlCkp%0Ac70%3D%0A)

## Select a Type of Tipping Policy

### Fixed Only / Percent Only

You can set the Small - Medium - Large tip option for both Fixed and Percent policy. This will be a fixed fare/percent regardless of fare amount.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/972640447/f617baf0d37a9c0c6609436a/IA5UT6cMIdsH8E83KuAbVSJDKITi6xFb9g.png?expires=1753820100&signature=2f5b1e1b645562d7c535b6470068836946716166a60d57f51809a91c6d4a0670&req=fSclEM1%2BmYVYFb4f3HP0gFq5n5Ebzij7f07%2BJhG9ROhuacIBlgPSJQyu4ysF%0AXWc%3D%0A)### Fixed, then Percent

You can set **Switch Amount** where the tipping policy switches from Fixed to Percent. When fares are below the amount, the Fixed option applies, and when above, the Percent option applies to fares.

## Apply the Policy to a Service

Lastly, go to **Services** to apply the tip policy to a service and save it:

1. Go to the **Service Planning** tab and select a **Service** you'd like to link to the Tipping Policy
2. Go to the Service settings by clicking **Edit Service**
3. Scroll down to see the **Tipping Enabled** option and switch the answer to **Yes**
4. **Select a tipping policy** you'd like to link
