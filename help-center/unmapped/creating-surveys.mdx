In this article, you will find answers to the following information:

How do I create surveys in Spare Eligibility?

How do I use survey templates?

How do I send my survey to customers?

## Building a New SurveyIn Spare 

Eligibility, you can easily build a survey form and distribute it to your riders to gather feedback on their experience.

To do so, go to **Spare Eligibility** and switch to the **Surveys** tab.
​

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490714/05f37f69e43c67ea6d966848/uU8q7ayaoYxvv5lrutlDoEfm0FgLrNXYzg.png?expires=1753820100&signature=82ce761d1748b9e535d9acdb8113aa9a76b54bc880d49ce94073f69c8654f389&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoBbFb4f3HP0gJhbcpeu2kbXjxXIhb4HupASEehLDul3Mv8Z9BPh%0AETM%3D%0A)

From there, you can either use a pre-built survey template or create a survey from scratch. 

**​Using Survey Templates**

- To use a Survey template, click "New Survey From Template". 

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490727/8a71dc19446aeadfbd539b1d/-zFtjeQxs0XS93KyxjUhFkGSziociMhR1A.png?expires=1753820100&signature=6a5494b14589877c2a4d4c4611c517e36815371550c7c7eb01fb8ed87ce652c9&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoNYFb4f3HP0gIWIVZhG%2BJ5hu16o%2FljZRp9INpRCdoIrMRus21f3%0A6oM%3D%0A)

Then, select one of the options available and click "Create Survey".

​

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490737/e7edf2ce1deb75ba367717a4/rWJThjEbebTOnmgIMV0OhV3rkmVrdaZmYA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=c6946429279b0f9feab0dc116c997ea461b1a72a83ab47e3ebbc1a06ea630157&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoJYFb4f3HP0gFFPXCIOwfH9F58U9k%2F%2Fi0SXwObbbIYSC28fulem%0Amwk%3D%0A)

You will then be redirected to the Survey Details page. Scroll down to the "Survey Fields" section to view current questions. You can archive or edit them as needed.
​

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490742/241db1f425e8992c78166575/we6vpeLYdJlTTh0v7J36xVpxokdGQgoChw.png?expires=1753820100&signature=cc67cf709400d055cc0ffd5fa3bed8c108d1ee7953add4dc8497034cda5f4b6f&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoVdFb4f3HP0gFZW47MQ%2FujLNkUzbBYR2I1gqstDFjGQCm9min1m%0AjyY%3D%0A)

You can also add questions by clicking "+Add Custom Questions". Select the question type and fill in the details.

​

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490751/891a4f3a2a7c07cb1357afe2/N3vbsOh2PBxxlJxoAATRv5GxcdTyFzV79A.png?expires=1753820100&signature=38a819056e483afc8ea879f4f348ee070848809db468f69dab6b1495da1be901&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoReFb4f3HP0gFrGEVybEXhYqu1RI01AlcrCsOHj1LRykI4y%2Fmkk%0AX5o%3D%0A)

**Building a Survey from Scratch**

- To start building a survey from scratch, click "+ Add Survey"

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490763/12853740bd0405db92f1d470/dxybKxS-x-rdxZKC7BN87cQxhUv98gg_ng.png?expires=1753820100&signature=7b85c46c751b6b81198e868feaedd32fdaf7117e364f22eb7ef837566dcc8410&req=fSYjEsB%2BmodcFb4f3HP0gHSZiAFt2kTBJfew9g%2B5x9R%2FngZe%2FCcB0V%2BImGcg%0A3tQ%3D%0A)

Fill in the general survey details and click "Create New Survey"

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490768/d5843c912616401456460089/8l2iNyims5JMH5X4D8RaU2ag0aOeAJqQfQ.png?expires=1753820100&signature=426e25cc55060a8f7038c1d1a9b6a1686726a835cc57968c1106c37ba6d732a9&req=fSYjEsB%2BmodXFb4f3HP0gEzI8%2Bx%2BXB9NpITbXi0KVT%2BjyluzHanWxLPGiEib%0AEHg%3D%0A)

You will then get to the Survey Details page. Click **"+Add Custom Questions" **to start building the survey form.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490771/fc845011a287ec9705741e20/tUKAEemd_YBg-PZd7h5fofSsZjlFQcXcvA.png?expires=1753820100&signature=ba2bd66a708054405b1f5ca66b69a88cfed2a6fecb070393517a419426034c9b&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoZeFb4f3HP0gO1WXEIS%2FvifY6XMwRtA9uaa00m5HOHFoHaGbMqS%0AztQ%3D%0A)

Select a question type and fill in the required details. Click "Create New Custom Fields" to save changes.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490779/881fd8cab9df32dd3eb16814/MUbOAvRRvQSBXsBP_vD3mRhye9c8ejmLOw.png?expires=1753820100&signature=9b0b65b4e91b92b5d8bbb14c16a50f0c0624efd261693f2cd487408e2fd48720&req=fSYjEsB%2BmoZWFb4f3HP0gEPhz6ur0eKmJMtzRi4e4UUJuXAq%2FW56ZNIRuhGo%0Apg0%3D%0A)

## Sending the Survey OutOnce the survey is built, you can easily send it out by clicking "Send Survey". 

Note that all riders will get invited to provide their input.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490783/2ebad1f8bde83e6fcf312b2b/2B_rBlKj0lRTTWYtd1-EiRTtYCHk2Aa--Q.png?expires=1753820100&signature=7aa7bb4157a3aba4fdc7badb31950fddcc5451a11c1b43e9bbb0a48dae9f814d&req=fSYjEsB%2BmolcFb4f3HP0gDmHFt7dj8g0Oa3bOXFgHdjSbMV1buCQ4WkjQ2sO%0AZSs%3D%0A)

You will be able to view your customers' feedback in the "Results" tab under the Survey Fields section.

![](https://spare-74fa64f80617.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/964490789/979ea980b3ff2b05f2b495d3/TgPqKPOczDvCQSQ3aNIKfixGpQD-Ff6rHQ.png?expires=1753820100&signature=1d82e8680f3144d4c85017585a3ec3a3632f238ba1ce987cbee8cedbc55cbfb7&req=fSYjEsB%2BmolWFb4f3HP0gNPbqWxmXWZz3v%2BDA60O35RDyoCdw770RX7%2BvgDd%0AmdA%3D%0A)
