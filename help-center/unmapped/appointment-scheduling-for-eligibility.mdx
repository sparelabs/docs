## About This Feature

Appointment Scheduling in Spare Eligibility simplifies managing interviews and in-person functional assessments for paratransit eligibility applications. This tool is ideal for managing teams of Eligibility Specialists and service administrators, ensuring streamlined coordination and efficient booking.

### Step-by-Step Setup Process

#### 1. Navigate to Case Setup

Go to the *Case Setup* section within Spare Eligibility.

#### 2. Select Case Template

Choose the appropriate case template, such as *Eligibility Application*.

#### 3. Add Appointment Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Appointments Tab">
    Click on the "Appointments" tab and select *Add Appointment*.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Template">
    Name your appointment template (e.g., "Test Interview").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Duration and Hours">
    Set the appointment duration and bookable hours (e.g., weekdays 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specify Restrictions">
    Specify unavailable hours or days to prevent booking conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Location">
    Provide a mandatory address for the appointment location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### 4. Assign Users

Add assignable users by selecting them from the user pool. 

<Note>
Users only show up in the list when their personal availability has been configured, either by an Admin or by the user themselves.
</Note>

### Access Appointment Booking

#### From the Case

1. Open the *Case* associated with the rider
2. Navigate to the *Appointments* tab and click *Book Appointment*
3. Select the desired template to view available slots

#### From the Appointments Page

1. Open the *Appointments* page
2. Click *Add Appointment*
3. Select the desired rider, case & other details

<Note>
It's only possible to select riders who have cases for them already.
</Note>

### Complete Booking

1. Confirm the date, time, and assignable user
2. Custom fields can be added for specific data collection (e.g., transportation details for riders attending assessments)

### Modify Appointments

#### From the Case

- Click *Actions* on a scheduled appointment to Cancel the Appointment
- Click on *Edit Appointment* to reschedule or assign a different user

#### From the Appointments Page

1. Click on the appointment you'd like to change
2. Click *Edit Appointment*

### Appointment Status

<Note>
The Appointment statuses are time-based and are automated except the Cancelled status.
</Note>

- **Booked**: Scheduled appointments
- **In Progress**: Appointments currently occurring
- **Completed**: Past appointments
- **Cancelled**: Cancelled Appointment

## Tracking Appointments

### Team View

Use the calendar view to track all appointments for all assignable users on that or any day.

### Filter Options

- View appointments for the next 12 months
- Choose between *List View* and *Calendar View* based on your needs and your role

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can admins set up availability for other users?">
    Yes, admins can configure availability for staff members to streamline the setup process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a staff member's availability changes?">
    Update the user's availability in their profile to reflect real-time changes. This ensures the staff remains assignable. You can also configure connecting to an external calendar such as Outlook to automate the process.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can notifications be triggered for appointments?">
    Yes, configure workflows to notify staff and riders about appointment updates, cancellations, or reminders. Please contact your Spare Representative to set this up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the system ensure fair distribution of appointments among users?">
    Appointments are assigned using a round-robin method to evenly distribute tasks and avoid overloading a single user.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I integrate external calendars like Outlook or Google?">
    Yes, users can link their external calendars to manage their availability seamlessly. Contact Spare Support for setup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
