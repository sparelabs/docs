<AccordionGroup>

    **Win When**: Transit-specific needs, FTA compliance required, multi-depot operations

    1. ‚úÖ **Transit-specific** - VMRS codes, FTA compliance built-in
    2. ‚úÖ **Facilities management** - Included (Fleetio doesn't have)
    3. ‚úÖ **Government ready** - Procurement friendly, public sector experience
    
    **Their Weakness**: Generic fleet tool trying to serve transit
    
    **üéØ Landmines to Plant**:
    - *"How does it handle FTA reporting requirements?"* - They'll struggle with transit compliance
    - *"Can it manage both fleet AND facilities?"* - They can't; requires separate system
    - *"What's your experience with government procurement?"* - Limited public sector refs
    - *"How do you handle multi-modal operations?"* - Built for trucks, not transit variety

    **Top 3 Advantages**:
    1. ‚úÖ **50% lower TCO** - No servers, IT overhead, or maintenance
    2. ‚úÖ **3-month deployment** - vs their 9-12 months
    3. ‚úÖ **Modern mobile app** - Mechanics actually use it

## üí¨ Objection Handling

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="We already have a maintenance system" icon="comment">
    **Response Framework**:
    1. Acknowledge: "I understand, many customers were in the same position..."
    2. Differentiate: "What they found was their system wasn't built for transit..."
    3. Prove value: "GCTD saved $200K annually after switching..."
    4. Next step: "Would you be open to a 15-minute demo focused on [specific pain point]?"
    
    **Supporting evidence**: GCTD case study, ROI calculator
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Our mechanics won't adopt new technology" icon="comment">
    **Response Framework**:
    1. Empathize: "That's a valid concern we hear often..."
    2. Address: "We designed the app specifically for mechanics - works offline, glove-friendly..."
    3. Proof: "GCTD saw 95% adoption in 30 days..."
    4. Support: "We provide on-site training and ongoing support..."
    
    **Supporting evidence**: Mobile app demo, training plan
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Implementation seems too complex" icon="comment">
    **Response Framework**:
    1. Simplify: "We've streamlined implementation to 12 weeks..."
    2. Support: "We handle all data migration and training..."
    3. Phasing: "We can start with just inspections..."
    4. Risk mitigation: "Pilot program available..."
    
    **Supporting evidence**: Implementation timeline, phased approach diagram
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No budget this fiscal year" icon="comment">
    **Response Framework**:
    1. Options: "Let me share a few approaches..."
    2. Start small: "EAM Lite at $50K entry point..."
    3. Timing: "Begin implementation now, payment next fiscal..."
    4. ROI: "Typically pays for itself in 6 months..."
    
    **Supporting evidence**: ROI calculator, payment terms flexibility
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
